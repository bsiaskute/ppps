Mentorship
At the beginning of my placement, I asked my manager for a business mentor. My mentor and I quickly established a close relationship by having bi-weekly meetings while covering a variety of professional and personal development topics, focusing on the PPP criteria. After each meeting, I would produce a brief notes document about the topic. As the Head of Business Collaborations, Tom had extensive experience across all parts of the business and could provide expertise and advice on an immense range of topics. He and my manager created a wonderful support system, which was especially crucial in the beginning.
Training
As my placement went on, I became very proficient in certain areas, such as dealer locator maintenance, photoshop and personalization. Together with the website coordinator we hosted a training session for the marketing managers from our subsidiaries on dealer locator maintenance and how to use our CMS (content management system).

Towards the end of my placement, I had to complete a handover of all my projects and provide training to the team members on any areas I specifically excelled in. I trained my team on image editing and optimization with Photoshop, provided A/B testing and personalization training.

Additionally, I had to complete handover to the next placement student and train a new off-shore website assistant to take over my projects.
