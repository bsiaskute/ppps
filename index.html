The Role
During the first 5 months at Triumph, I worked in the website team. In November, I expressed interest in shadowing the CRM manager. In January, I was offered the opportunity to work 1 day a week in the CRM team, which later on increased to 2 and 3 days.
Key Responsibilities
  • Website content editing. This includes improving the usability, design, and conversion of the company website
  • Using data from the attribution model to deliver Website personalization with pop-ins and personalized website content
  • Producing Reporting/Analytics as requested by the Website Manager
  • Optimise the digital customer journey based on analytics
  • Content management - resizing, editing and organizing images
  • Quality control, scheduling and sending local market emails provided by subsidiaries
  • Assisting in migrating email journeys to Microsoft Dynamics 365, building emails, creating journeys, performing testing
  • Producing reports of email performance
  • Overseeing CRM activity while the CRM Coordinator is out of office
  • Updating old email journeys from a visual and journey flow perspective
